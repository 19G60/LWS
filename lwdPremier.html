Our modern classrooms, well-equipped labs, and digital resources create an interactive environment where every learner thrives. Pastoral Care

At Lakewood Premier School, we believe that every child learns best when they feel safe, supported, and valued. Our pastoral care program focuses on emotional growth, kindness, and strong connections between learners and teachers. Through guidance, mentorship, and open communication, we create a welcoming environment where students develop confidence, resilience, and a deep sense of belonging within the Lakewood community.

Extracurricular Activities

We believe education extends far beyond academics. Our co-curricular programs inspire creativity, teamwork, and personal growth through clubs, sports, arts, and innovation activities. Learners are encouraged to discover their interests, nurture their talents, and develop the confidence to shine both inside and outside the classroom.

Each learner is expected to choose at least one off the following programs.
