Application Guidelines

Thank you for choosing Lakewood Schools, where we nurture excellence and character in every learner. To complete your application, please prepare the following documents in soft copy for upload and hard copy for submission to the school. A Birth Certificate and Parent/Guardian ID are required to submit your application. Additional documents may be uploaded later in the process. Our Enrollment Officers are available to guide and support you every step of the way.

Step 1: Pre-Admission Documents
Please ensure you have the following documents ready before starting your application:

  1. Copy of Immunization Certificate – Reception to Preschool only
  2. Copy of Child’s Birth Certificate
  3. Copy of Parent’s/Guardian’s valid Passport and Visa (if not a Kenyan citizen)
  4. Copy of Pupil’s valid Pass/Visa (if not a Kenyan citizen)
  5. Passport-size Photographs – 2 for the student and 1 for each parent/guardian
  6. Copy of Mother’s ID Card/Passport and 1 Passport-size Photo
  7. Copy of Father’s ID Card/Passport and 1 Passport-size Photo
  8. Certified Copy of the Last Report from Previous School (Optional)
  9. NEMIS Number and Assessment Number (for CBC Curriculum)
  10. Certified Copy of KPSEA Results (Grade 7 & 8 CBC Curriculum)

Step 2: Admission Process
  1. Complete the online application form available on our website or at the school office.
  2. Submit the required documents along with the application form.
  3. Await communication from our Admissions Team regarding the next steps, which may include an interview or assessment.
