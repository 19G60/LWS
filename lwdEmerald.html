Our Approach to Curriculum Delivery

At Lakewood Komarock Schools, we take pride in offering a dynamic, learner-centered approach that nurtures every child’s potential from Kindergarten to Grade 9. Guided by the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), our program blends academic excellence with holistic development, emphasizing creativity, critical thinking, and practical learning. Our classrooms are enriched with modern digital learning tools alongside engaging resources such as educational toys, flashcards, charts, and creative cutouts — all designed to inspire active participation, collaboration, and curiosity. With dedicated teachers, interactive lessons, and technology-integrated learning experiences, we empower our learners to build confidence, independence, and strong character while achieving remarkable academic growth and preparing for lifelong success in a digital world.

Pastoral Care

we understand that the transition from home to school can be a significant step, especially for our younger learners. That’s why we provide a warm, nurturing environment that supports every child’s emotional and social well-being. Our pastoral care system ensures that each learner feels valued, safe, and supported throughout their school journey. With a dedicated team of compassionate and qualified teachers, we offer consistent guidance, active listening, and emotional support — fostering positive relationships between learners, teachers, and parents, and helping every child feel a true sense of belonging within the Lakewood family.

Community Engagement

At Lakewood Komarock Schools, we value the power of community in shaping a child’s learning journey. We actively partner with parents and the local community to build meaningful connections and foster a supportive environment that enriches every learner’s growth and well-being.

Our Key Features: +
  • Regular parent-teacher meetings and workshops to keep parents informed and involved in their child’s progress.
  • Community service projects that teach learners the value of giving back and making a positive impact.
  • Open communication channels between teachers, parents, and community members to address concerns and celebrate successes together.
  • Inviting local experts and organizations to share knowledge and experiences with our learners, broadening their horizons.
  • Organizing family-friendly events and activities that strengthen the bond between school, home, and community.
  • Encouraging learners to take pride in their community through cultural celebrations, environmental initiatives, and social responsibility programs.
