About Lakewood Schools/

History

Lakewood Schools began its inspiring journey in 2010 at Lakewood Komarock with just 20 kindergarten learners — a humble beginning rooted in a big vision: to provide quality, holistic education that shapes both the mind and character. Guided by a passion for excellence, innovation, and inclusivity, the school rapidly grew, giving rise to Lakewood Premier, Lakewood Emerald, and Lakewood Vantage School — each expanding our legacy of nurturing confident, responsible, and high-achieving learners. Today, Lakewood Schools stands as one of Kenya's leading education networks, offering world-class facilities and a dynamic CBC curriculum designed to empower students to excel academically and thrive in life. Join us and become part of a community where potential is discovered, nurtured, and celebrated.

Achievements

At Lakewood Schools, we celebrate milestones that reflect our pursuit of excellence. Over the years, our students have excelled in academics, co-curricular activities, and national assessments under the CBC curriculum. Beyond academics, our learners have represented the school in sports, arts, and innovation competitions, earning recognition both locally and internationally.
