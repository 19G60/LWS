Welcome to Lakewood School

At Lakewood School, we believe every child deserves an education that nurtures their unique talents and prepares them for a successful future. Our commitment to academic excellence, combined with a focus on character development, has made us one of Kenya's leading educational institutions.

With over 15 years of experience, five thriving campuses, and a dedicated team of educators, we continue to set the standard for quality education that transforms lives and builds communities.

Our Pillars

At Lakewood Schools, our pillars guide everything we do, shaping our identity and driving our mission to nurture well-rounded, future-ready learners.

Remarkable Academic Growth

We are committed to nurturing learners who consistently achieve remarkable academic growth through innovative teaching, personalized support, and a culture of continuous improvement.

Pastoral Care

We prioritize the well-being of every learner through dedicated pastoral care that fosters emotional growth, moral guidance, and a supportive school community.

Affordability

We are dedicated to providing quality, holistic education that remains accessible and affordable to all families without compromising on excellence.
